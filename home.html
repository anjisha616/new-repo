Trump says Israel-Iran ceasefire ‘in effect’ after deadly overnight strikes


Israel-Iran ceasefire: Trump said early Tuesday that a ceasefire between Israel and Iran was now “in effect.” Iranian and Israeli media earlier said a ceasefire had begun, but confusion swirled over the precise details.


Latest strikes: Israel said it identified multiple missiles launched from Iran early Tuesday morning with one slamming into a residential building in the south that left five dead. Iranian authorities reported Israel launched fresh attacks on Iran’s capital Tehran overnight and killed at least nine in a strike on a city in the north.
